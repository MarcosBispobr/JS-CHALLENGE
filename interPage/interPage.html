Learn how to use the Fullture To do List

  • First, you will type the name of the task on the box.

  • Afterword, you may click on the button to add the task on the list

  • Subsequently, the task will be disponible to mark as made or to delete

  • So, if you want to delete the task, click on the task name

  • But, if you want mark the task as made, you may click on the button named by "Made"

Now, it's time to get your daily organized.
